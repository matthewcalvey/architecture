04 — Aerodynamic Response

Wind Profiling

The solar-leaned form from the previous step is reshaped for aerodynamic performance. As buildings grow taller, wind forces increase with the square of velocity. The form responds by narrowing at the base (reducing the stagnation zone), constricting at a Venturi neck (accelerating flow for ventilation), and tapering at the crown (reducing peak wind loads).
